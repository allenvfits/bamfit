Flexibility & Recovery

STRETCHING

What Is PNF Stretching?

PNF (Proprioceptive Neuromuscular Facilitation) is the stretching of the golgi tendon — one of the most effective flexibility and recovery techniques available. Anthony assists every stretch manually, allowing you to reach ranges of motion you simply cannot achieve on your own.

Most clients feel a measurable difference after a single session. Increased range of motion is immediate and compounds — each session builds on the last.

Increased range of motion
Improved flexibility
Enhanced athletic performance
Better posture and alignment
Improved neuromuscular coordination
Reduced risk of injury
