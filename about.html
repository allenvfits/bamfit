Anthony B. Iacono • Founder

BAM FIT — By Any Means Fitness was built on a simple but powerful belief: everyone deserves to feel good in their body. Not just athletes. Not just people with hours to spare. Everyone.

Anthony B. Iacono specializes in six core disciplines — strength training, functional fitness, weight loss, mobility, assisted PNF stretching, and everyday lifestyle coaching — because he understands fitness is not one-size-fits-all.

The goal is always longevity. Not a six-week crash result that fades by month three. Anthony coaches toward a lifetime of feeling strong, moving well, and being fully capable of whatever life demands.
