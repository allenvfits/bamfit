Anthony B. Iacono • Founder

BAM FIT — By Any Means Fitness was built on a simple but powerful belief: everyone deserves to feel good in their body. Not just athletes. Not just people with hours to spare. Everyone.

Anthony B. Iacono specializes in six core disciplines — strength training, functional fitness, weight loss, mobility, assisted PNF stretching, and everyday lifestyle coaching — because he understands fitness is not one-size-fits-all. Your body, your goals, and your life are unique. Your training should be too.

From the first-time client who has never stepped foot in a gym to the seasoned athlete who needs to move better and recover faster — Anthony meets you exactly where you are and builds from there. No judgment, no shortcuts, no cookie-cutter plans.

The goal is always longevity. Not a six-week crash result that fades by month three. Anthony coaches toward a lifetime of feeling strong, moving well, and being fully capable of whatever life demands of you.

Besides looking physically good and feeling better mentally, regular exercise does something most people do not fully appreciate — it protects you from the diseases and conditions that cut lives short and reduce quality of life for millions.
